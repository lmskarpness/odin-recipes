Simple Coffee

Description

Coffee is delicious! This recipe delivers a simple, affordable alternative to what you would buy in a coffee shop!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cold brew coffee
  • 2 (1 gram) packets of granular scurolose sweetener (like Splenda)

Steps

  1. Mix milk, coffee, and sweetener together in a glasss until sweetener is dissolved.
